Baked Buffalo Wings

Well living in New York State, you know i have to like the hometown favorite; Buffalo Wings! Well you dont need a fryer for these wings. Just bake them in the oven for a little over an hour and have some wings at home!

INGREDIENTS

Wings

  • 4 lbs chicken wings
  • 5 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt

Sauce

  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degree F
  2. Toss wings in baking powder
  3. Bake 30 minutes on bottom rack of oven
  4. Mix butter, Frank's Red Hot, brown surgar and salt together.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes on 425 degrees F on top rack of oven
  6. Remove from oven and toss in sauce
  7. Serve
